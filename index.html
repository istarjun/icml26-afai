About

Financial markets represent one of the most challenging frontiers for ML: constant distribution shift, low signal-to-noise ratios, and high-stakes decisions. As the industry shifts toward agentic systems—autonomous entities capable of reasoning and planning— the gap between empirical success and theoretical understanding widens.

  • Robustness & OOD: continual learning under non-stationarity
  • Safety & Regulation: verifiable, certified robustness in high-stakes domains
  • Agentic systems: planning, tool use, multi-agent coordination

XForecast Challenge

Overview

A community challenge on news-grounded financial forecasting, designed to test how modern AI—from reasoning agents to multimodal foundation models— can deconstruct complex market narratives.

  • Dataset: 5 years of Reuters News (LSEG) aligned with market price/volume
  • Task: out-of-sample forecasting for 2024–2025
  • Prize: $4,500 prize pool (LG AI Research)
  • Timeline: Apr–Jun 2026 (TBD exact dates)
